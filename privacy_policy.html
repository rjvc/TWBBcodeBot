Privacy Policy for TWBBcodeBot

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect personal information. However, through your interaction with the bot, the following types of data may be processed:

  • Discord User Information: When you interact with TWBBcodeBot, we may access your public Discord username, user ID, and avatar. This information is used to personalize your experience with the bot and to respond to commands.
  • Messages: Any messages sent to the bot, including any commands or other interactions, are processed to fulfill your requests. These messages are not stored or logged beyond the scope of the current interaction.
  • Bot Commands: When you use specific bot commands, such as /choose or others, the data related to these interactions (such as server and world selection) may be stored temporarily for the purposes of providing services within the bot.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information in the following ways:

  • To provide and improve the functionality of TWBBcodeBot.
  • To respond to user commands and interactions.
  • To track interactions for internal purposes, such as error logging and performance improvements.

3. Data Retention

We do not store personal data permanently. Data related to user interactions is stored temporarily during the session and deleted after the interaction is completed. We do not keep logs of conversations or personal data unless explicitly required for functionality.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell your personal data with third parties. Any data shared with third parties (e.g., for error reporting or integration with other services) is anonymized and is only used to improve the bot's performance.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Discord API: TWBBcodeBot operates through the Discord API, which is used to facilitate communication and actions between the bot and users. For more information on Discord's data practices, you can review their Privacy Policy.
  • External Links: Our bot may link to external websites or services. These links are not under our control, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those sites.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request the deletion of any data related to your interactions with the bot.
  • Contact us for clarification or concerns regarding how your data is handled.

If you wish to delete your data or have any other privacy-related concerns, please reach out to us through our support channels.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the bot, and the updated Privacy Policy will be available at the same URL where you first accessed it.
